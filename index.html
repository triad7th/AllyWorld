Privacy Policy

Last updated: Apr 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AllyFast (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our mobile application, AllyFast (“App”).

Information We Collect

AllyFast is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. Our App does not require account creation or collect any personal data. All data, including fasting records and preferences, is stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

Since no personal data is transmitted or stored on external servers, we use only the information stored locally on your device to provide a personalized fasting experience. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Your fasting records and settings remain on your iPhone. AllyFast does not use any cloud storage, ensuring your data remains private and secure. However, the security of your data also depends on the security settings of your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
